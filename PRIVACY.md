# Public Sharing And Privacy Notes

This repository is intentionally sanitized for public portfolio use.

## Safe To Include

- General Omeka S implementation notes.
- Metadata and content model examples using fake/sample records.
- Public-facing UX and information architecture notes.
- General Reclaim Hosting workflow explanations.
- Approved screenshots after permission is granted.
- Diagrams made specifically for public sharing.

## Not Included

- Full theme folders.
- Raw archive datasets.
- Client-private research materials.
- Server credentials or configuration.
- Admin usernames, passwords, or access details.
- UH internal documents.
- Unapproved screenshots.
- Private client communications.

## Sharing Approach

The goal is to show platform implementation, documentation, metadata thinking, and UX reasoning while protecting client materials and project-sensitive files.
